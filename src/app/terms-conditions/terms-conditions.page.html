About the Chef

Toeknee Tee

"Ella's Kitchen" is inspired by the eats of International Pop Star Toeknee Tee(US). Named after his grandma Ella, whom he spent many summers with in Nashville, TN, this collection of delectible recipes spans numerous Continents. Toeknee Tee grew up in the deep south before moving abroad to locations such as Australia and Europe, where his taste buds evolved from their southern flavor base. 10 years without a Walmart or Publix is likely to change anyones pallete, so taste the world with his latest Recipes.
